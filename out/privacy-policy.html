Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide during account creation and verification, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID, biometric liveness checks, address verification
  • Financial Data: Bank account connections (via Plaid), work email domain
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device fingerprints
  • Usage Data: Login history, feature usage, geolocation patterns
  • Transaction History: Peer-to-peer transaction outcomes, feedback, and dispute records

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Calculate Reliability Score: Combine verification signals to generate your trust score and TrustBadge
  • Verify Identity: Prevent fraud and ensure platform integrity across marketplaces and peer-to-peer transactions
  • Provide Service: Enable account access, authentication, and features
  • Share with Partners: Provide your score and TrustBadge (not raw data) to authorized marketplace partners and individuals you choose to share with
  • Improve Service: Analyze usage patterns and optimize scoring algorithms
  • Communicate: Send important updates, security alerts, and notifications

3. Data Sharing

We share your information in the following ways:

Marketplace Partners & Users

We share your reliability score, grade, and TrustBadge status with authorized partners and individuals you choose to share with. We do NOT share raw personal data (ID photos, bank details, biometric data) without your explicit consent. When you share your TrustBadge during a peer-to-peer transaction, the other party sees only your verification status and score.

Third-Party Services

  • Persona - Identity verification (KYC/AML)
  • Plaid - Bank account verification
  • Supabase - Database and authentication
  • Vercel/Railway - Hosting infrastructure

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, subpoena, or to protect our rights, prevent fraud, or ensure user safety.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using TLS/SSL and AES-256
  • Access Control: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication for staff
  • Secure Infrastructure: SOC 2 compliant hosting (Supabase/Vercel/Railway)
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Monitoring: 24/7 system monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Data Isolation: Raw identity data stored separately from application data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., for tax, legal, fraud prevention, or compliance purposes). Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytics and algorithm improvement.

Your TrustBadge and reliability score become inactive immediately upon account deletion and are no longer accessible to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Object: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Revoke Consent: Withdraw consent for data sharing with specific partners

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@chekkd.com

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Authentication, session management, security
  • Analytics: Usage statistics, performance monitoring (anonymized via Google Analytics)
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit functionality.

8. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with third-party providers that have their own privacy policies:

  • Persona Privacy Policy
  • Plaid Privacy Policy
  • Supabase Privacy Policy

We recommend reviewing these policies to understand how your data is handled by our partners.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@chekkd.com.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

Note: We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or prominent in-app notification. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
