Shaun Kimball

Founder & CEO

Shaun Kimball is a repeat founder operating at the intersection of digital identity, blockchain, and fintech. He previously founded Nomyx, a tokenization-as-a-service platform enabling real-world asset verification and smart identities, and before that launched SOS Supply, a national PPE supplier during the height of COVID-19.

Across each venture, he encountered the same systemic friction: people want to transact, but they lack portable, provable trust. That insight became the foundation for CHEKKD.

As Founder & CEO, Shaun leads the company's mission and vision. His leadership blends product intuition, brand clarity, and mission-driven execution, grounded in the belief that trust should not be assumed or gamed -- it should be earned, proven, and universally accessible.

Jon Sanderson

Co-Founder & COO

Jon Sanderson is a growth and operations executive with more than 15 years of experience scaling performance-driven marketing organizations across complex B2B and infrastructure-focused technology companies.

He has held executive leadership roles spanning decentralized cloud storage, big-data CX platforms, marketing automation, and enterprise SaaS -- including overseeing multi-division marketing and sales organizations with hundreds of employees and global footprints. His work has driven significant ARR growth, reduced churn, expanded upsell revenue, and built high-performing demand engines from the ground up.

At CHEKKD, Jon leads operations and go-to-market strategy, translating technical innovation into scalable growth. With deep fluency in data-driven performance, automation systems, and buyer psychology, he ensures the company's growth engine is disciplined, measurable, and aligned with long-term enterprise value creation.

Matthew Schwen

Co-Founder & CTO

Matthew Schwen is a veteran infrastructure and automation leader with two decades of experience building and operating large-scale networks for global enterprises, including Oracle, Humana, Adobe, Symantec, and The Trade Desk.

At Humana, he led network automation engineering and implemented AI-driven tooling that centralized workflows and reclaimed over 1,000 engineering hours per month. He previously co-founded and served as CTO of PortalCX, where he helped lead product strategy, recruiting, fundraising, and software development.

As CTO of CHEKKD, Matthew architects the scalable, resilient systems that power the Trust Score and verification platform. Widely recognized for his work at the intersection of AI and infrastructure, he brings production-grade automation expertise and enterprise-level security rigor to ensure the company's technology foundation is built for durability, scale, and mission-critical trust.
