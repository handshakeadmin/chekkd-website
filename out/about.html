How Identity Verification Works

Our identity verification process combines multiple data points to create a comprehensive trust profile. Users complete a quick verification that includes government ID validation, bank verification, and employment verification through Plaid. The result is a verified TrustBadge that can be shared across any platform.

For businesses and platforms, CHEKKD offers seamless API integration that adds verification capabilities without disrupting user experience. Our system processes thousands of verifications per second while maintaining bank-level security and full compliance with data protection regulations.

Who Uses CHEKKD?

Marketplace platforms integrate CHEKKD to reduce fraud and build buyer confidence. When users see a verified TrustBadge, conversion rates increase by up to 40% because trust removes friction from transactions.

Individual sellers and buyers use CHEKKD to protect themselves in peer-to-peer transactions. Before meeting a stranger from Craigslist or accepting a rental application, smart users verify identity first.

Hiring platforms and employers rely on CHEKKD for streamlined candidate verification. Our instant checks replace weeks of waiting, helping companies onboard faster while maintaining compliance.

Why Choose CHEKKD?

Online scams cost consumers over $30 billion annually, and marketplace fraud accounts for $12 billion of that loss. CHEKKD exists to change these statistics by making verification accessible, affordable, and instant.

Our platform stands out through speed (verification in seconds, not days), portability (one verification works across multiple platforms), and privacy (users control what information is shared). We believe trust should be universal, not siloed.
